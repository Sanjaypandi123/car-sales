INFORMATION ABOUT TATA

The Tata Group is an Indian multinational conglomerate founded in 1868 by Jamsetji Tata. Headquartered in Mumbai, it operates in over 100 countries across various sectors like IT, steel, automotive, energy, and consumer products, with key companies including Tata Steel, Tata Motors, and Tata Consultancy Services (TCS).

TATA SAFORI

The Tata Safari is a premium SUV from Tata Motors, known for its bold design, strong performance, and advanced safety features. It is built on Tata’s OMEGARC platform, derived from Land Rover’s D8 architecture, ensuring strength and stability. The Safari offers a powerful 2.0L Kryotec diesel engine, 6-speed manual or automatic transmission, and seating for 6 or 7 passengers. Key features include Panoramic Sunroof, Touchscreen Infotainment System with Android Auto & Apple CarPlay, ADAS.

Rs. 5.99 - 9.10 Lakh

TATA ALTROZ

The Tata Altroz is a premium hatchback with petrol, diesel, and CNG options, featuring both manual and automatic transmissions (AMT for petrol, DCT for some petrol variants). Key details include standard safety features like six airbags, a 360-degree camera on higher trims, a 26.03 cm infotainment system with wireless connectivity, and a revamped interior with a new steering wheel and digital instrument cluster.

Rs. 6.30 - 10.51 Lakh

TATA BOLT

The Tata Bolt was a 5-seater hatchback, available with 1.2L petrol or 1.3L diesel engines, both mated to a 5-speed manual transmission. Key features included a multi-drive mode on the petrol engine, a Harman infotainment system, and a ground clearance of 165 mm. While it's no longer in production, it was considered a value-for-money car with a focus on ride comfort and features for its time.

Rs. 5.33 - 7.96 Lakh

TATA CURVV

The Tata Curvv is a premium coupe-SUV offered in both electric (EV) and internal combustion engine (ICE) versions, featuring a stylish design and modern technology. It comes with various engine options, including petrol and diesel for the ICE models, and is equipped with features like a Level 2 ADAS suite, a 5-star Bharat NCAP safety rating, and a voice-activated panoramic sunroof.

Rs. 9.66 - 18.85 Lakh

TATA TIAGO

The Tata Tiago is a compact hatchback offered with petrol, CNG, and electric (EV) powertrain options. Key features include a 1.2L petrol engine with 85 bhp, and a 1.2L CNG engine with 74 bhp in the iCNG variant. The petrol model has a mileage of around (19 kmpl), while the CNG version achieves approximately (28.06 km/kg). It is a 5-seater with a 170mm ground clearance, and is available with both manual and automatic transmissions

Rs. 4.57 - 7.82 Lakh

TATA PUNCH

The Tata Punch is a compact SUV available with 1.2-liter petrol or CNG engines, with either manual or automatic transmissions. Key specifications include a 187 mm ground clearance, 366-liter boot space, and a 5-star GNCAP safety rating. It offers a 5-seater capacity and comes in various trims, featuring technologies like a 7-inch infotainment system on higher variants.

Rs. 5.50 - 9.30 Lakh
