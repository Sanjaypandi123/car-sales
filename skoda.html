INFORMATION ABOUT SKODA

Škoda is a Czech automobile manufacturer, established in 1925 and now a subsidiary of the Volkswagen Group. It has a long history, originally founded as Laurin & Klement in 1895, and is known for producing vehicles with features like the "Simply Clever" solutions. The brand has a global presence and operates its own subsidiary, Škoda Auto India, which has been in operation since 2001.

SKODA KODIAQ

The Skoda Kodiaq is a 7-seater SUV that comes with a 2.0-liter turbocharged petrol engine, producing 204 PS of power and 320 Nm of torque, paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. It features a spacious interior with multiple comfort and convenience options like three-zone climate control and smart dials, along with safety features like nine airbags and a 360-degree camera.

₹39.99 lakh

SKODA OCTAVIA

The Škoda Octavia is a 5-seater sedan with a 1984cc petrol engine available with an automatic transmission, producing 261.49bhp and 370Nm of torque in its performance-oriented RS model. It features a 7-speed dual-clutch gearbox, 100km/h in 6.4 seconds, and offers luggage space of 600 litres with rear seats up, expandable to 1,555 litres when folded. Safety features include up to 10 airbags, fatigue detection, and automatic parking.

₹49.99 Lakh

SKODA SUPERB

The Skoda Superb is a premium sedan with a 1984cc, 4-cylinder turbocharged petrol engine that produces 187.74 bhp and 320 Nm of torque. It features a 7-speed automatic transmission (DCT) with paddle shifts and offers a mileage of approximately 15.1 kmpl. Key interior features include ventilated front seats, a 12-speaker Canton sound system, a large touchscreen, and a leather-wrapped steering wheel, while the exterior is characterized by an aerodynamic design, LED headlights, and alloy wheels.

Rs. 54 Lakh
