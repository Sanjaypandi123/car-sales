INFORMATION ABOUT LEXUS

Lexus is the luxury vehicle division of the Japanese automaker Toyota Motor Corporation, founded in 1989. It is known for producing premium cars and SUVs, with models like the ES, NX, RX, and LX, and is marketed in over 90 countries. The brand emphasizes high-performance vehicles, a focus on quality craftsmanship, and customer-centric technology.

LEXUS ES

The Lexus ES is a luxury midsize sedan that is available as a hybrid, with two main variants being the 300h Exquisite and 300h Luxury. Key features include a 2.5L hybrid powertrain, a choice of 6-speed automatic or E-CVT transmission, and advanced safety features like 10 airbags and stability control. It is equipped with modern technology such as a 12.3-inch multimedia screen, smartphone connectivity, and an available 17-speaker Mark Levinson audio system.

₹62.65 Lakh

LEXUS GS

The Lexus GS was a luxury sports sedan that ran from the 1990s until production ended around 2020. It is known for its rear-wheel-drive platform, driver-focused design, and luxurious features like a large multimedia display and premium sound systems. Available with different engine options, including V6, V8, and hybrid powertrains, it was positioned as a more dynamic and driver-oriented alternative to the front-wheel-drive ES sedan.

₹35.00 Lakh

LEXUS LX

The Lexus LX is a luxury SUV with a focus on power, capability, and premium features. It is powered by a 3.3-liter V6 twin-turbo diesel engine, features an aluminum hood and roof for a lower center of gravity, and includes advanced technologies like fingerprint authentication for the push-start button. Available in variants like the 500d Urban and 500d Overtrail, the LX offers a high-strength chassis for off-road performance and a luxurious interior with features like climate control, ventilated seats, and a 360-degree camera.

₹3.31 - ₹3.44 Crore

LEXUS RC

The Lexus RC is a luxury sports coupe offered in 2025 models as the RC 300, RC 350, and high-performance RC F. Key features across the lineup include modern technology like a 10.3-inch touchscreen display, Apple CarPlay/Android Auto integration, and standard Lexus Safety System+ 2.5. The RC F, the final edition, features a powerful 5.0L V8 engine, carbon fiber components, and track-focused performance enhancements.

₹2.74 Crore
