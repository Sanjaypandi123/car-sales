INFORMATION ABOUT BUGATTI

Bugatti cars are known for their high-performance, luxury vehicles, with recent models like the Chiron featuring a \(1,479\) bhp, \(8.0\) L quad-turbo W16 engine and an automatic transmission. Other models like the Veyron are also iconic, featuring a \(1,001\) bhp engine and a top speed exceeding \(400\) km/h. Bugatti cars are characterized by handcrafted interiors, innovative design, and the use of premium materials, with a seating capacity of two for most models.  

BUGATTI CHIRON

The Bugatti Chiron is a two-seater hyper sports car known for its extreme performance, luxury, and advanced aerodynamics. It is powered by a massive 8.0-liter, quad-turbocharged W16 engine producing over 1,500 horsepower and 1,600 Nm of torque, allowing it to accelerate from \(0-100\) km/h in just \(2.4\) seconds. The car features a sophisticated active aerodynamic system and a luxurious, high-tech interior.  

₹19.21 Cr to ₹28.40 Cr

BUGATTI DIVO

The Bugatti Divo is a mid-engine hypercar developed for track-focused handling, named after French racer Albert Divo. It features a 1,500 PS (1479 bhp) 8.0-liter quad-turbo W16 engine, a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, and is 35 kg lighter than the Chiron. Key features include 35 kg more downforce than the Chiron, a maximum speed of 380 km/h (236 mph), and a faster lap time on the Nardo handling circuit.

₹41 crore

BUGATTI TYPE 35

The Bugatti Type 35 is a highly successful and iconic race car from the 1920s and 1930s, designed by Ettore Bugatti and introduced in 1924. It's considered the most successful race car of all time, with over 2,000 victories, and is praised for its elegant design, innovative engineering, and excellent handling. The car was also a commercial success, with the Grand Prix model sold to private racers, and it was unique in being usable on both public roads and racetracks.

$2.9 million
