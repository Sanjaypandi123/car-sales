INFORMATION ABOUT KIA

Kia is a South Korean multinational automobile manufacturer that was founded in 1944. It began as a maker of bicycles and motorcycles before becoming a full-fledged car company and is now part of the Hyundai Motor Group. Kia is a global mobility brand focused on producing sustainable solutions, and its brand slogan is "Movement that inspires".

KIA SELTOS

The Kia Seltos is a compact SUV known for its upscale design, loaded interior, and multiple powertrain options. It is available with a 1.5-liter naturally aspirated petrol, a 1.5-liter turbo-petrol, and a 1.5-liter diesel engine, paired with manual or automatic transmissions. Key features include dual 10.25-inch displays, a heads-up display, ventilated front seats, and in higher trims, Level 2 ADAS.

Rs. 5.99 - 9.10 Lakh

KIA CARNIVAL

The Kia Carnival is a luxury MPV available in India with a 2.2L diesel engine and an 8-speed automatic transmission. It features a 7-seater (2+2+3) configuration, advanced safety features like ADAS and 8 airbags, and a luxurious interior with options like power-sliding doors, a dual sunroof, and high-end seats. The car offers a large boot space of up to 1600+ liters with the third row folded.

Rs. 6.30 - 10.51 Lakh

KIA NIRO

The Kia Niro is a hybrid or electric crossover SUV available in various models, including a hybrid, plug-in hybrid, and a fully electric version (EV). Key features include a 1.6L GDI petrol engine for hybrid models, a 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). The Niro EV offers a 65 kWh battery with a potential range of around 270 miles, though its fast-charging capabilities are slower than newer models.

Rs. 5.33 - 7.96 Lakh

KIA SPORTAGE

The Kia Sportage is a compact SUV available with various engines, including gasoline and hybrid options. Key features often include a 1999 cc diesel engine (in some markets) producing 181 bhp, a 2.5L gasoline engine with 187 horsepower in other models, and hybrid variants. It has a seating capacity of 5, comes with front-wheel or four-wheel drive, and is equipped with modern features like touchscreen infotainment, cruise control, and various safety technologies.

Rs. 9.66 - 18.85 Lakh

KIA SORENTO

The Kia Sorento is a mid-size SUV available in gasoline, hybrid, and plug-in hybrid models, featuring a 7-seat configuration and advanced technology. It competes with other large SUVs and offers options like all-wheel drive and modern features such as a panoramic sunroof, large infotainment displays, and various driver-assistance systems. Specifics like engine size, transmission, and features vary by trim level and market.

Rs. 4.57 - 7.82 Lakh

KIA FORTE

The Kia Forte is a compact sedan available with a 2.0-liter engine (147 hp) or a 1.6-liter turbocharged engine (201 hp) in premium trims. It features a continuously variable transmission (CVT) or a 6-speed manual/7-speed dual-clutch automatic, depending on the trim. The Forte is equipped with standard features like an 8.0-inch touchscreen, smartphone integration, and various safety systems. Note that Kia has discontinued the Forte and replaced it with the new K4 model.

Rs. 5.50 - 9.30 Lakh
