INFORMATION ABOUT LAMBORGHINI

Lamborghini is an Italian manufacturer of high-performance luxury sports cars, founded by Ferruccio Lamborghini in 1963 and based in Sant'Agata Bolognese. It is currently a subsidiary of the Volkswagen Group, acquired in 1998. Lamborghini is known for its distinctive design and powerful engines, with current models including the V12 plug-in hybrid Revuelto, the V8 plug-in hybrid Urus SE Super SUV, and the V8 plug-in hybrid Temerario.

LAMBORGHINI AVENTADOR

The Lamborghini Aventador is a discontinued, high-performance V12 supercar with a 2-door, 2-seater coupe/convertible body style, known for its aggressive, fighter-jet-inspired design and powerful engine. It features a naturally aspirated 6.5L V12 engine and an independent shifting rods (ISR) automatic transmission that sends power to all four wheels, with performance variants like the SVJ and Ultimae producing up to 780 CV (574 kW). Key specifications include a top speed of around 355 km/h (220 mph) and a 0-100 km/h time of about 2.8 seconds for the Ultimae.

Rs. 5.01 Crore - 9 Crore

LAMBORGHINI DIABLO

The Lamborghini Diablo is a mid-engine, rear-wheel or all-wheel drive sports car produced from 1990 to 2001, succeeding the Countach. It is known for its high performance, with a mid-mounted V12 engine and top speeds exceeding 200 mph. Numerous variations were produced, including the all-wheel drive VT and the more powerful, track-focused GT and GTR models.

$281,787

LAMBORGHINI GALLARDO

The Lamborghini Gallardo is a mid-engine sports car produced from 2003 to 2013, known for its V10 engine, all-wheel-drive (in most models), and combination of high performance with daily usability. It was available as a coupé and a convertible (Spyder) and came with either a 6-speed manual or an E-gear transmission. The Gallardo's engine evolved from a 5.0L V10 in the first generation to a 5.2L V10 in the second, with horsepower increasing from around 500 hp to up to 570 hp in some special editions.

Rs. 1.55 Crore - 3.06 Crore

LAMBORGHINI URUS

The Lamborghini Urus is a high-performance luxury SUV known for its blend of speed, opulent design, and versatility. It features a powerful twin-turbo V8 engine, available in petrol-only and plug-in hybrid variants, and offers advanced technology with multiple driving modes and a luxurious interior. Key features include a dual-touchscreen center console, advanced safety systems, and a capacity to seat five passengers.

Rs. 4.18 - 4.57 Crore
