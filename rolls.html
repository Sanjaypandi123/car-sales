INFORMATION ABOUT ROLLS-ROYCE

Rolls-Royce is a British multinational company that manufactures aerospace and defense systems, and also has a separate luxury car company under the same brand. The aerospace and defense side, Rolls-Royce Holdings plc, designs and builds power systems for aviation and other industries. The luxury car division, Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Limited, is a subsidiary of BMW and manufactures high-end vehicles.

ROLLS-ROYCE BOAT TAIL

The Rolls-Royce Boat Tail is a bespoke, ultra-luxury car with a unique "yacht" design and a price tag of around £20 million, making it one of the most expensive new cars ever made. Developed by the Rolls-Royce Coachbuild division, it is a limited-edition vehicle with only three planned to be built, each customized to its owner. It shares its chassis and a 563 hp twin-turbo V12 engine with the Phantom, but features a completely unique body and a host of luxurious features.

₹205 crore

ROLLS-ROYCE SPECTRE

The Rolls-Royce Spectre is the brand's first all-electric car, a luxury two-door coupe that features a 102 kWh battery providing an estimated WLTP range of up to 530 km. It is powered by dual electric motors, produces 585 PS and 900 Nm of torque, and accelerates from 0-100 km/h in 4.5 seconds. Key features include an aerodynamic design, a highly connected and intelligent interior with multiple digital screens, and advanced suspension for a smooth ride.

₹7.5 crore

ROLLS-ROYCE GHOST

The Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxury sedan known for its refined V12 engine, opulent interior with high-quality materials, and advanced technology, including a digital driver display, advanced audio system, and head-up display. It features a 6.75-liter twin-turbo V12 engine, an 8-speed automatic transmission, and all-wheel drive, delivering a smooth and powerful ride. The interior emphasizes comfort and craftsmanship, with options for bespoke customization.

₹8.95 crore

ROLLS-ROYCE CULLINAN

The Rolls-Royce Cullinan is a luxury SUV powered by a 6.75L V12 petrol engine, available with an automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. It features a double wishbone front and five-link rear suspension, self-levelling air suspension, and a stereo camera to scan the road ahead and adjust the ride proactively. The Cullinan also includes a four-wheel steering system for improved maneuverability and stability

₹10.50 crore to ₹12.25 crore
