INFORMATION ABOUT JAGUAR

A jaguar is a large cat species native to the Americas, known for its powerful bite and spotted coat. The name is from the Tupi and Guarani languages and means "true, fierce beast". They are apex predators that live in rainforests and other habitats, hunting a wide variety of prey and playing a crucial role in their ecosystems. Information about this topic can also refer to the automotive brand or the SEPECAT aircraft.

JAGUAR F PACE

The Jaguar F-Pace is a luxury compact SUV known for its blend of performance, style, and technology. It is available with various engine options, including 2.0-liter petrol and diesel, both often paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Key features include an 11.4-inch infotainment touchscreen, a 12.3-inch digital driver's display, panoramic roof, and advanced safety systems like multiple airbags and stability control.

₹72.90 lakh

JAGUAR F TYPE

The Jaguar F-Type is a luxury sports car available as a two-seater coupe or convertible, known for its performance and agile handling. It features petrol engines ranging from \(1997\) cc to \(5000\) cc, an automatic transmission, and a 100 mm ground clearance. Key technologies include the Electronic Active Differential, Torque Vectoring by Braking, and the 12.3-inch Interactive Driver Display. 

₹97.93 Lakhs

JAGUAR XF

The Jaguar XF is a 5-seater luxury sedan with a range of petrol and diesel engines, an automatic transmission, and a 4-door configuration. Key features include a turbocharged petrol engine option, automatic climate control, and various comfort and safety features. It offers a balance of performance, technology, and luxury in a sedan package.

₹49.78 lakh to ₹76.00 lakh

JAGUAR XKR

The Jaguar XKR is a high-performance grand tourer, produced with a supercharged 5.0-liter V8 engine and typically featuring a 6-speed automatic transmission. It is a luxury car known for its combination of power, agility, and a sophisticated interior, available in both coupe and convertible body styles. The XKR has a rear-wheel-drive (RWD) setup and comes in a 2+2 seating configuration.

₹1.37 crore to ₹1.88 crore
