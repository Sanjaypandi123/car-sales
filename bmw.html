INFORMATION ABOUT BMW

BMW is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1929, with a deep history in motorsport. The company is known for high-performance vehicles, with its current headquarters in Maranello, Italy. Ferrari spun off from its parent company, Fiat Chrysler Automobiles, in 2016 to become a public company.

BMW M3

The BMW M3 is a high-performance sports sedan known for its powerful engines and sporty handling. The current generation features a 3.0L twin-turbo inline-6 engine, available with either a 6-speed manual or a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, and a rear-wheel-drive (RWD) or all-wheel-drive (M xDrive) system. It offers various models, such as the M3 Competition Sedan, which has an engine producing up to 510 hp (375 kW) and can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in as little as 3.5 seconds.

Rs. 1.47 Cr

BMW M4

The BMW M4 is a high-performance sports coupe known for its powerful engines, sharp handling, and driver-focused design. It is available in several models, like the M4 Competition and M4 CS, which feature a twin-turbocharged inline 6-cylinder engine, with the Competition model producing over 500 horsepower and the CS variant offering more power and lightweight components. Key features include an 8-speed M Steptronic transmission, available M xDrive all-wheel drive, and high-tech interior elements like a large iDrive display.

Rs. 1.52 - 1.79 Crore

BMW M7

There is no current model named "BMW M7"; instead, BMW offers "M" versions of the 7 Series, such as the i7 M70 xDrive, an all-electric performance luxury sedan, and the past M760Li xDrive, which featured a V12 engine. These high-performance models blend luxury with the athletic nature of M cars, featuring powerful engines or electric drivetrains, advanced suspension, and luxurious interiors.

₹1.79 - ₹1.82 Crore

BMW X5

The BMW X5 is a luxury SUV with various engine options, including a 3.0L turbocharged petrol in the xDrive40i and a 3.0L turbocharged diesel in the xDrive30d. Key features include xDrive all-wheel drive, an 8-speed automatic gearbox, and high-end technology like a 12.3-inch infotainment screen, a digital instrument cluster, and a panoramic sunroof. It also features premium materials, a split tailgate for cargo flexibility, and a range of safety systems.

Rs. 93.70 Lakh - 1.05 Cr
