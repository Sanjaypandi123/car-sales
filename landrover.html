INFORMATION ABOUT LAND ROVER

Land Rover is a British brand of four-wheel-drive, off-road capable luxury SUVs owned by the Indian company Tata Motors through its subsidiary Jaguar Land Rover (JLR). Originating from the Rover Company in 1948, the brand is now known for its premium sport utility vehicles like the Range Rover, Defender, and Discovery. JLR builds Land Rovers in the UK, Brazil, China, India, and Slovakia.

FREE LANDER

The Land Rover Freelander was an SUV that is now discontinued, but was known for its off-road capability, on-road comfort, and a spacious interior. The Freelander 2, the most recent version, featured a 2.2-liter diesel engine with an automatic transmission, all-wheel drive, and a 5-seat capacity. It also came with modern amenities like ABS, airbags, and optional premium audio systems.

Rs. 34.95 Lakh - 47.87 Lakh

RANGE ROVER

The Range Rover is a luxury SUV with various engine options, including petrol and diesel mild-hybrid variants. It features an 8-speed automatic transmission, all-wheel drive (AWD), and electronic air suspension for comfort and off-road capability. Key features include a spacious cabin, premium materials, advanced driver-assistance systems, a multi-zone climate control, and sophisticated infotainment options.

Rs. 2.31 Crore

LAND ROVER SPORTS

The Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a five-seater luxury SUV that offers a range of engine options, from mild hybrid diesel to powerful V8 petrol, with performance-focused variants like the SV achieving 0-100 km/h in 3.9 seconds. It features on-road and off-road capabilities, with a 6D suspension system in the SV model for improved handling, and can be equipped with premium features like massage seating and a large infotainment screen. The price varies by model and region, with the Range Rover Sport starting from approximately ₹1.38 Crore in India.

Rs. 1.38 - 2.75 Crore

LAND ROVER VELAR

The Land Rover Velar is a luxury mid-size SUV known for its modernist design, pioneering innovation, and all-terrain capability. It features advanced technology like the Pivi Pro infotainment system, a 3D Surround Camera, and a clean cabin air system with PM2.5 filter. Engine options typically include a 2.0L turbocharged petrol engine with an automatic transmission, and its design emphasizes sophisticated elegance, with features like flush door handles and advanced Pixel LED headlights.

Rs. 79.24 - 83.91 Lakh
