INFORMATION ABOUT LOTUS

Lotus is a British sports car manufacturer known for its lightweight, performance-oriented vehicles, founded in 1948 by Colin Chapman. The company is now owned by China's Geely Automotive Group and produces models like the Emira, Emeya, and Eletre. Lotus cars emphasize engineering, handling, and performance, often built around the philosophy of "simplify, then add lightness".

LOTUS ELETRE

The Lotus Eletre is an electric hyper-SUV available in multiple trims with a 112 kWh battery, a range of up to 600 km (WLTP), and high performance, with models offering over 900 horsepower. Key features include a 15.1-inch touchscreen, digital driver and co-driver displays, a premium KEF audio system, and autonomous driving technology with LiDAR sensors. It is positioned as a premium alternative to SUVs like the Jaguar I-Pace and BMW iX, and as an electric competitor to cars like the Lamborghini Urus S.

Rs. 2.55 - 2.99 Crore

LOTUS EMIRA

The Lotus Emira is a high-performance sports car that replaces the Evora, Exige, and Elise. It is available with a 3.5L supercharged V6 engine or a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine. The V6 version offers manual or automatic transmissions and has a 0-60 mph time of 4.2 seconds, while the turbo-four uses an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission.

Rs. 3.22 Crore
