INFORMATION ABOUT MAHINDRA

Mahindra & Mahindra is a large, publicly listed Indian multinational conglomerate headquartered in Mumbai, with a diverse portfolio spanning the automotive, farm equipment, IT services, and financial services sectors. Founded in 1945, it is known for its SUVs, commercial vehicles, and being the world's largest tractor company by volume. The Mahindra Group includes over 100 subsidiaries in various industries.

MAHINDRA XUV700

The Mahindra XUV700 is a 7-seater SUV with a commanding road presence and a feature-rich interior, available with both petrol and diesel engines. Key specs include a 2.0L turbo petrol engine (197 bhp/380 Nm) and a 2.2L turbo diesel engine with two power outputs (182 bhp/450 Nm or 155 bhp/360 Nm). It offers manual or automatic transmissions, an optional all-wheel-drive (AWD) on diesel models, and a 60-liter fuel tank.

Rs. 10.61 - 18.43 Lakh

MAHINDRA THAR ROXX

The Mahindra Thar ROXX is a lifestyle SUV with a 5-seater capacity, available in both petrol and diesel engine options with manual and automatic transmissions. It features modern amenities like a touchscreen, panoramic sunroof, and LED lighting, while also offering robust off-road capability through its 4x4 variants and electronic locking differential.

Rs. 6.30 - 10.51 Lakh

MAHINDRA BOLERO

The Mahindra Bolero is a robust, diesel-powered, 7-seater SUV known for its ruggedness and utility. It is available with a 1.5L diesel engine, producing around (75) bhp and (210) Nm of torque, and is paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. Key features include a front-independent coil spring suspension, rear-leaf spring suspension, and front disc/rear drum brakes. Newer variants offer safety features like dual airbags and ABS with EBD. 

Rs. 5.33 - 7.96 Lakh

MAHINDRA SCARBIO

The Mahindra Scorpio is a rugged SUV available in both standard and newer Scorpio N variants, known for its bold, boxy design and off-road capability. Key features include diesel and petrol engine options, manual and automatic transmissions, and seating for up to 7 passengers. Recent models offer advanced features like touchscreen infotainment, multiple airbags, and electronic stability control, with prices ranging from approximately ₹12.98 Lakh to ₹24.17 Lakh (ex-showroom) depending on the model and variant.

Rs. 9.66 - 18.85 Lakh

MAHINDRA XUV-3XO

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is a 5-seater subcompact SUV available with 1.2L turbo-petrol and 1.5L diesel engines. It features a modern design with a dual-tone option, a panoramic sunroof, and advanced tech like Level 2 ADAS on higher variants. With a 5-star BNCAP safety rating, it also offers features such as dual 10.25-inch displays and a Harman Kardon sound system.

Rs. 4.57 - 7.82 Lakh

MAHINDRA THAR

The Mahindra Thar is a rugged SUV with diesel and petrol engine options, manual and automatic transmissions, and both 2WD and 4WD configurations. Key features include a ladder frame chassis, a 226mm ground clearance, and a 4-seater capacity. The petrol engine is around (2.0L), while diesel engines include a (1.5L) and a (2.2L) option. It is known for its off-road capability and has a 4-star Global NCAP safety rating. 

Rs. 5.50 - 9.30 Lakh
