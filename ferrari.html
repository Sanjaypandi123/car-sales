INFORMATION ABOUT FERRARI

Ferrari is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1929, with a deep history in motorsport. The company is known for high-performance vehicles, with its current headquarters in Maranello, Italy. Ferrari spun off from its parent company, Fiat Chrysler Automobiles, in 2016 to become a public company.

FERRARI GTC4LUSSO

The Ferrari GTC4Lusso is a 4-seater shooting-brake with all-wheel drive, available with either a 6.2-liter V12 or a 3.9-liter V8 engine in the GTC4Lusso T variant. It features a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, with the V12 having 681 hp and the T producing 610 hp. It is equipped with a unique four-wheel-drive system, four-wheel steering, and a large central touchscreen infotainment system.

₹4.26 - ₹4.97 Crore

FERRARI PORTOFINO

The Ferrari Portofino is a 2+2 hardtop convertible grand touring sports car featuring a front-mounted, twin-turbo V8 engine, an automatic 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, and rear-wheel drive. It was introduced as the successor to the California T and can accelerate from 0-100 km/h in approximately 3.5 seconds, with a top speed exceeding 320 km/h. The updated Portofino M includes performance enhancements, like a new 8-speed gearbox and a "Race" mode on the Manettino.

Rs. 6.30 - 10.51 Lakh

FERRARI ROMA

The Ferrari Roma is a front-engine, 2+2 grand tourer coupe that pays homage to the Italian "la dolce vita" of the 1950s and 60s. It is powered by a 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 620 horsepower and 760 Nm of torque, mated to an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. Key features include a sleek design, an all-digital 16-inch instrument display, and a touchscreen infotainment system with a separate display for the passenger.

Rs.5.40 Cr

LA FERRARI

The LaFerrari is a hybrid hypercar from Ferrari, featuring a 6.3-liter V12 engine and an electric motor, for a combined output of 963 CV hp. It has a top speed of over km/h mph and can accelerate fromtokm/h (62 mph) in under 3 seconds. The car is also known for its advanced aerodynamics, which were developed using F1 technology.
