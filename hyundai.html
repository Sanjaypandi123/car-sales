INFORMATION ABOUT HYUNDAI

Hyundai is a South Korean multinational automotive manufacturer, founded in 1967, that produces a wide range of vehicles, including cars, SUVs, and electric models. The company is part of the Hyundai Motor Group and is known for its global presence, innovation, and its luxury brand, Genesis. Its global vision is \"Progress for Humanity,\" which emphasizes sustainable mobility and customer-focused solutions.

HYUNDAI CERTA

The Hyundai Creta is a compact SUV available with petrol and diesel engines, automatic and manual transmissions, and a 5-seater capacity. It features a dual-screen setup, wireless charging, and a panoramic sunroof on higher trims. Key dimensions include a length of 4,330 mm, width of 1,790 mm, and height of 1,635 mm, with a 2,610 mm wheelbase.

Rs. 10.61 - 18.43 Lakh

HYUNDAI KONA

The Hyundai Kona is a small SUV available in various powertrains, including electric, hybrid, and gasoline. The Kona Electric offers an estimated 452 km range and features include a 100 kW motor, a 39.2 kWh battery, and various charging options. Gasoline versions come with options like a 2.0L engine or a more powerful 1.6L turbocharged engine, with the high-performance Kona N featuring a 2.0L turbocharged engine.

Rs. 6.30 - 10.51 Lakh

HYUNDAI NEXO

The Hyundai Nexo is a mid-size hydrogen fuel cell electric vehicle (FCEV) SUV that emits only water, offering a quiet and smooth driving experience similar to a battery electric vehicle. The all-new model features a significantly improved powertrain with a total output of 190 kW, a projected driving range of up to 826 km (WLTP), and a fast refilling time of about five minutes. It includes advanced safety and driver-assistance systems, a spacious interior, and technologies like e-Handling, a Smart Regenerative System, and a route planner to find hydrogen stations.

Rs. 5.33 - 7.96 Lakh

HYUNDAI PALISADE

The Hyundai Palisade is a flagship SUV known for its large size, premium features, and seating for up to 9 people. It is expected to launch in India around March 2026, with an estimated price of ₹40-50 lakh. Key features include a choice of powertrains (with a new hybrid option for the 2026 model), a spacious and luxurious interior with multiple comfort and convenience features, and advanced safety technology.

Rs. 9.66 - 18.85 Lakh

HYUNDAI SONATA

The Hyundai Sonata is a mid-size sedan with different versions available globally, including gasoline and hybrid models. Key features often include a 5-seater capacity, a range of engine options, and either manual or automatic transmissions. Modern versions also feature advanced safety systems like Forward Collision-avoidance Assist and in-car technology such as an 8-inch infotainment unit with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto.

Rs. 4.57 - 7.82 Lakh

HYUNDAI TUCSON

The Hyundai Tucson is a premium SUV available with 1997 cc diesel and 1999 cc petrol engines, both paired with an automatic transmission. It is a 5-seater SUV featuring modern technology like a 10.25-inch HD infotainment system, Hyundai Bluelink connected car services, and an advanced driver-assistance system (ADAS). Key features include a range of safety systems, multiple drive modes, and optional 4WD on some diesel variants.

Rs. 5.50 - 9.30 Lakh
