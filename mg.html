INFORMATION ABOUT MG

MG, originally Morris Garages, is a British automotive brand founded in 1924 that is now owned by the Chinese company SAIC Motor. After its acquisition by SAIC, MG has evolved into a modern auto-tech company focusing on a range of vehicles, including electric cars, with a significant presence in the Indian market through the joint venture JSW MG Motor India. Its Indian operations are based in Halol, Gujarat, and the company's product line includes models like the MG Hector, MG Astor, and MG ZS EV.

MG HECTOR

The MG Hector is a mid-size SUV available with 1.5L turbo-petrol and 2.0L turbo-diesel engines, offering both manual and automatic transmissions. Key features include a 14-inch touchscreen, connected car technology, and Level 2 ADAS in higher variants, with seating options for five, six, or seven passengers across its different trims like the Style, Shine Pro, Select Pro, Smart Pro, and Sharp Pro.

Rs. 10.61 - 18.43 Lakh

MG ASTOR

The MG Astor is a 5-seater compact SUV available with a 1.5-litre petrol engine in either manual or CVT automatic transmission. Key features include a 10.1-inch touchscreen, an AI-powered voice assistant, and a panoramic sunroof. It is 4,323 mm long, 1,809 mm wide, and 1,650 mm high, with a 48-litre fuel tank and a 488-litre boot space.

Rs. 6.30 - 10.51 Lakh

MG CYBERSTER

MG Cyberster Price - Images, Colours & Reviews - CarWaleThe MG Cyberster is a two-door, dual-motor electric sports car with a 77 kWh battery, 510 PS (503 bhp) power, and 725 Nm of torque. It can accelerate from 0-100 km/h in 3.2 seconds, has an official range of 580 km (CLTC cycle), and features scissor doors, a foldable soft-top, and a fighter-jet-inspired cockpit with a unique dashboard setup.  

Rs. 5.33 - 7.96 Lakh

MG COMET-EV

The MG Comet EV is a small, two-door, four-seater electric car designed for city driving, with a 17.3 kWh battery providing an estimated range of 230 km. It has a 41.42 bhp permanent magnet synchronous motor, an automatic transmission, and takes 7 hours to charge to 100% from a 3.3kW charger. Key features include a compact footprint for easy parking, LED headlamps, and a rearview camera.

Rs. 9.66 - 18.85 Lakh

MG GLOSTER

The MG Gloster is a full-size, three-row SUV with 6 or 7-seat configurations, powered by a 2.0L diesel engine with either a single-turbo (2WD) or twin-turbo (4WD) setup, both paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. It features a large, commanding presence, a luxurious interior with a 12.3-inch touchscreen, and a suite of advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS).

Rs. 4.57 - 7.82 Lakh

MG WINDSOR-EV

The MG Windsor EV is an electric vehicle in India featuring a 52.9 kWh battery offering a 449 km range. It includes advanced features like Level 2 ADAS, a 15.6-inch touchscreen, a panoramic glass roof, and Vehicle-to-Load (V2L) capabilities. The car also has a spacious interior with 135-degree reclining rear seats, a nine-speaker audio system, and R18 alloy wheels.

Rs. 5.50 - 9.30 Lakh
