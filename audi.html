INFORMATION ABOUT AUDI

Audi is a German luxury vehicle manufacturer that is a subsidiary of the Volkswagen Group, known for its sporty and high-quality cars with a focus on "Vorsprung durch Technik" (Vorsprung durch Technik). The company's emblem is four rings, representing the 1932 amalgamation of four German companies: Audi, DKW, Horch, and Wanderer. Audi produces a wide range of vehicles, including sedans, SUVs, and coupes, and has a significant global presence.

AUDI A6

The Audi A6 is a luxury sedan known for its sleek design and advanced features, including a dual-touchscreen MMI infotainment system, a Bang & Olufsen sound system, and driver assistance technologies. It is powered by a 1984 cc petrol engine and comes with an automatic transmission. Key specs include a 14.11 kmpl mileage, automatic climate control, and a 5-star Euro NCAP safety rating.

Rs. 63.74 - 69.89 Lakh

AUDI A8

The Audi A8 is a luxury sedan known for its advanced technology, powerful performance, and premium interior. Key specifications include a 3.0L twin-turbocharged V6 engine with a 48V mild-hybrid system, an 8-speed automatic transmission, and quattro all-wheel drive. It features a luxurious interior with dual touchscreens, an MMI infotainment system, and a high-end sound system, with the A8 L configuration offering extended legroom.

Rs. 1.27 - 1.54 Crore

AUDI Q3

The Audi Q3 is a compact luxury SUV known for its sporty design, upscale interior, and technology like the virtual cockpit and Quattro all-wheel drive. In India, it is available with a 2.0-liter turbo-petrol engine and automatic transmission. The vehicle has a five-seater capacity and features like configurable ambient lighting and a 10-inch MMI touchscreen.

Rs. 43.07 - 52.31 Lakh

AUDI Q5

The Audi Q5 is a 5-seater premium compact SUV known for its powerful 2.0-liter turbocharged petrol engine, standard quattro all-wheel drive, and a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. It features a sporty design, a spacious and practical cabin, and a variety of modern amenities like Audi's Virtual Cockpit, a large touchscreen infotainment system, and advanced safety features. In India, it is offered in two main variants: the Premium Plus and the Technology.

Rs. 64.74 - 69.86 Lakh
