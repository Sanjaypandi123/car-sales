INFORMATION ABOUT NISSON

Nissan is a Japanese multinational automobile manufacturer headquartered in Yokohama, Japan, founded in 1933. The company sells vehicles under its own name and the Infiniti brand, and has a history of producing products from cars to rocket engines for spacecraft. Nissan is known for its wide range of vehicles, from sedans and SUVs to electric cars, and a focus on innovation. 

NISSON GT-R

The Nissan GT-R is a high-performance sports car known for its 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine, which produces over 560 horsepower and 637 Nm of torque. It features a 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, an all-wheel-drive system, and can accelerate from \(0-100\) km/h in about 3 seconds. The car is a 2+2 coupe with a seating capacity for four and has been a long-standing icon, earning the nickname "Godzilla".

NISSON R33 SKYLINE GT-R

The R33 Skyline GT-R is a high-performance Japanese sports car, produced by Nissan from 1995 to 1998, known for its twin-turbocharged 2.6L RB26DETT inline-six engine and advanced ATTESA E-TS all-wheel-drive system. It features a stiffer, more rigid chassis and improved aerodynamics over its predecessor, the R32. The base model produces around 276 bhp (206kW)

NISSON SKYLINE (R32) GT-R

The Nissan Skyline GT-R (R32) is an iconic sports car from 1989 known for its twin-turbocharged, 2.6-liter RB26DETT inline-6 engine, which produced about 276-280 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. It features a 5-speed manual transmission and an all-wheel-drive system, with a curb weight of around 1,430 kg (3,150 lbs). The R32 GT-R was developed to dominate Group A racing and was highly successful, leading to its nickname "Godzilla".

SPORTS CAR NISSON Z

The Nissan Z is a rear-wheel-drive sports car with a 3.0L twin-turbo V6 engine, available in Sport, Performance, and NISMO trims. It offers either a 6-speed manual or 9-speed automatic transmission and features a modern design inspired by its heritage. Key performance upgrades include a limited-slip differential, sport-tuned suspension, and powerful brakes, particularly on the higher-end models.
