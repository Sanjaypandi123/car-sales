INFORMATION ABOUT VOLKSWAGEN

Volkswagen is a German automobile manufacturer based in Wolfsburg, Germany, and is the flagship brand of the global Volkswagen Group. The company, founded in 1937, is known for producing the iconic VW Beetle, and it also owns other major brands like Audi, Porsche, Skoda, and Lamborghini. The company has a worldwide presence with a focus on innovation, sustainability, and electrification.

VOLKSWAGEN TAIGUN

The Volkswagen Taigun is a 5-seater SUV with two turbo-petrol engine options (1.0L) and (1.5L) and is known for its high safety rating, achieving 5 stars in Global NCAP crash tests for both adult and child occupants. It features modern technology like a 10.25-inch touchscreen with wireless connectivity, ventilated front seats, and a 50-liter fuel tank. Key specifications include a 188mm ground clearance and a 385-liter boot space.

Rs. 10.61 - 18.43 Lakh

VOLKSWAGEN BEETLE

The Volkswagen Beetle is a 4-seater hatchback with a petrol engine, available with an automatic transmission. It features a 1395 cc engine that produces up to 148 bhp, offers a mileage of around 17.68 kmpl, and has a ground clearance of 136 mm. The Beetle's dimensions are 4278mm in length and 1808mm in width.

Rs. 6.30 - 10.51 Lakh

VOLKSWAGEN GOLF

The Volkswagen Golf is a performance hatchback, known for its sporty design and advanced features, particularly in its high-performance GTI variant. The GTI is powered by a 2.0L turbocharged petrol engine, producing 261 bhp and 370 Nm of torque, and is paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. Key features include a 12.9-inch infotainment system with wireless connectivity, 3-zone climate control, a sunroof, and ADAS technology.

Rs. 5.33 - 7.96 Lakh

VOLKSWAGEN PASSAT

The Volkswagen Passat is a 5-seater sedan that was last available in India with a 1968cc diesel engine and a 6-speed automatic transmission, offering a mileage of 17.42 kmpl. Key features include a Dynamic Chassis Control, full LED lighting, and massage and heating functions for the driver's seat. It has been discontinued in the Indian market, with its last recorded price ranging from approximately ₹30.21 to ₹33.22 Lakh.

Rs. 9.66 - 18.85 Lakh

VOLKSWAGEN POLO

The Volkswagen Polo is a 5-seater hatchback known for its premium build quality and performance, available with 1.0L MPI and 1.0L TSI petrol engines. It offers manual and automatic transmission options, a mileage of around 17.8-18.2 kmpl, and features like a 280-liter boot space, a 4-star ASEAN safety rating, and connectivity options like Android Auto and Apple CarPlay.

Rs. 4.57 - 7.82 Lakh

VOLKSWAGEN TOUAREG

The Volkswagen Touareg is a large, 5-seater SUV with a history of both gasoline and diesel engine options, available with automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. While the model has been discontinued in some markets, newer versions feature advanced technology like air suspension, a panoramic sunroof, and digital cockpit screens, alongside extensive safety features.

Rs. 5.50 - 9.30 Lakh
